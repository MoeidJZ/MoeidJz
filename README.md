## 🌌 About Me
I am a researcher working at the intersection of quantum computing hardware and microwave engineering. My work focuses on advancing quantum hardware capabilities, specifically through the development and optimization of high-coherence superconducting resonators and quantum devices. I combine heavy electromagnetic modeling with hands-on laboratory automation and cleanroom fabrication insights to build the next generation of quantum processors.

## 🛠️ Research Focus & Technical Expertise

### **Quantum Hardware & RF Engineering**
* **Superconducting Quantum Circuits:** Design and characterization of superconducting resonators (including advanced materials like Tantalum Carbonitride) and Josephson junction-based devices.
* **RF/Microwave Components:** High-frequency electromagnetic modeling, design, and cryogenic microwave measurements.
* **Simulation Tools:** Multiphysics and high-frequency electromagnetic field solvers (ANSYS HFSS, Sonnet, etc.).

### **Data Analytics & Lab Automation**
* **Languages:** Python, MATLAB, LaTeX
* **Instrument Control:** Developing automated measurement suites using **QCoDeS**, PyVISA, and custom hardware interfaces for cryogenic and RF test setups.
* **Data Science & Visualization:** Processing and analysis of large experimental datasets using `pandas`, `NumPy`, `SciPy`, and `matplotlib`.
